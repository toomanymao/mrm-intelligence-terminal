Live Price Widgets
BTC / USD Spot
Real-time Bitcoin price in US dollars, streamed tick-by-tick via WebSocket. Also shows the 24h percentage change, color-coded green (up) or red (down).
Source: Binance WebSocket — wss://stream.binance.com
Sentiment Circuit
The Crypto Fear & Greed Index on a 0–100 gauge. Scores below 25 signal extreme fear (historically a buying opportunity). Scores above 75 signal extreme greed (market may be overheated).
Source: Alternative.me Fear & Greed API
Market Structure
Gold Parity Matrix
Bitcoin's market cap expressed as a percentage of gold's estimated total market cap (~$16.5T). Tracks BTC's long-term journey toward — and potentially beyond — gold parity.
Source: Calculated from live BTC price × circulating supply
Dominance
Bitcoin's share of total global crypto market capitalisation. High dominance (>50%) means capital is concentrated in BTC. A falling reading often signals rotation into altcoins (alt-season).
Source: On-chain aggregator
OTC Liquidity Flow
Estimated Bitcoin reserves held by Over-The-Counter (OTC) desks used by institutional buyers. When OTC supply dries up, large buyers are forced onto open exchanges — a supply-shock signal that has historically preceded price moves.
Source: On-chain aggregator estimates
On-Chain Indicators
MVRV Z-Score
Market Value to Realised Value Z-Score. Compares Bitcoin's market cap to its "realised cap" — the sum of what every coin last moved was worth at the time. A Z-Score below 2 historically marks undervalued territory. Above 5 has coincided with every major market top.
Source: Modelled from live price + realised cap approximation
Halving Countdown
Every ~210,000 blocks (~4 years), Bitcoin's block subsidy is cut in half. This programmatically reduces new supply. We are in Epoch 5 (3.125 BTC per block). The next halving will cut this to 1.5625 BTC.
Source: Calculated from known halving dates + 10-min block average
Holdings & Flows
Institutional Flow
Bitcoin treasury holdings of major public companies and spot ETFs. In period views (7d, 30d, etc.), shows net BTC bought or sold. Green = accumulation, Red = distribution.
Source: Public filings, BitcoinTreasuries.net, ETF issuer disclosures
Sovereign Treasury Holdings
Bitcoin held by national governments — through seizures, strategic reserves, or legal tender policy. Sovereign accumulation signals state-level recognition of BTC as a strategic reserve asset.
Source: Public government disclosures, BitcoinTreasuries.net
Supply Distribution
Breakdown of circulating supply by holder type: retail, institutions, sovereign wallets, Satoshi's known untouched coins (~1.1M BTC), and estimated permanently lost coins. In filtered views, shows net flows for the selected period.
Source: On-chain clustering analysis + public data
Miner Inventory & Hashrate Heat
Bitcoin held by major mining pools and listed mining companies, plus total network hashrate in exahashes per second (EH/s). Miner selling pressure is a key short-term indicator — miners selling heavily can signal local tops.
Source: On-chain miner wallet tracking, public mining company reports
Wisdom on the Wire
A curated rotating quote from Bitcoin pioneers, economists, and builders — Satoshi Nakamoto, Hal Finney, Michael Saylor, Jeff Booth, and others. A new one appears on each page load.
Source: Curated quote library
